How to make great pizza!



This recipe will teach you how to make a delicious pizza! Feel free to add your own ingredients and make the recipe your own!

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Anything else you'd like!
  1. Start by preheating your oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Roll out your dough so that it is flat.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly across the surface of the dough.
  4. Sprinkle cheese across the pizza. Use as much as you'd like!
  5. Add whatever additional ingredients you'd like!
  6. The oven should be ready! Put your pizza inside!
  7. Bake for 15 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
